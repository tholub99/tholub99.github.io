Stratos Farm

Stratos Farm is a tower-defense type strategy game where your goal is to grow crops while defending them from hungry rabbits. Working with a team of artists I was responsible for all of scripts and integrating all the completed assets into Unity. Concept and game design was done in collaboration with the art team who provided the completed assets such as 3D Models, 2D UI, and textures.

The Game

Play the game by either downloading the build or playing our webGL version
(There are some issues with the webGL build as the game was not designed for it)
